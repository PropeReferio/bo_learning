X - backspace
. - repeat last command
dB - delete back a word
"0 - contains the most recent yanked text, not deleted
"" - contains most recently deleted text
"F - append to f register
cc - change whole line
g$~ - change case until end of line
g~~ - change case of whole line
gUw - Make whole next word uppercase.
tb - search linewise to just before the next b
* - go to the next occurence of highlighted word. Awesome for variables.
# - same, but reverse
ci] - change everything inside the []
ca] - change everything inside the [], and also the [] itself. The other text objects work the same way, such as ciw, caw.
